# A석 — 벽난로 프롬프트

조립: `[SPACE] + [LOCKS] + 아래 목적별 + (시간·날씨 변형) + [NEGATIVE]`

## A-1 접근 컷 (establishing)

```text
Approaching the fireplace corner at slow walking pace from the rug, camera height
150cm settling toward 100cm: the grey sandstone fireplace with a low, long-burning
fire, the walnut rocking chair with ochre linen cushion angled 25 degrees toward
the flames, the small round side table with the chipped enamel mug at its right,
willow log basket at the hearth's left. Firelight (1900K, #E8944A) pools low on the
floor and chair legs; the rest of the room falls into soft readable shadow. In the
right background, out of focus, the north window and the far lamp glow remain
faintly visible.
```

## A-2 착석 고정 구도 (seated master)

```text
Static frame from 45 degrees behind-left of the rocking chair, camera height 100cm,
distance 1.6m: chair and fireplace share the frame, fire at left, the round table
with mug at lower right. Flames small and steady, brightness variation subtle.
Soot stain fixed above the fireplace opening. No motion except gentle flame flicker
and, rarely, a settling ember.
```

## A-3 디테일 컷

```text
Close frame at f/4 of the round walnut side table: chipped white enamel mug with two
faint ring stains on the wood, firelight catching the mug's rim from the left,
rocking chair armrest entering the frame's edge. Quiet, warm, unstaged.
```

## 좌석 전용 유의

- 불은 항상 "낮고 오래 탄 불". 장작 4~6개, 큰 화염 금지.
- 낮 컷에서도 이 구석은 방에서 가장 어둑하다 (북창광이 여기까지 오며 약해진다).
