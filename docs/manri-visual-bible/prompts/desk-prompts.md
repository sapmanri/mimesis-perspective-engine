# B석 — 책상 프롬프트

조립: `[SPACE] + [LOCKS] + 아래 목적별 + (시간·날씨 변형) + [NEGATIVE]`

## B-1 접근 컷 (establishing)

```text
Walking straight from the entrance toward the desk under the north window, camera
height 150cm settling toward 115cm: the tall sash window grows in the upper frame,
grey-white diffuse daylight (5000K) even on the oak desktop. Matte black typewriter
centered with a half-typed page in its carriage, brass lamp with green glass shade
at the left rear corner, ceramic cup of pencils, ink bottle, leather desk pad.
Orderly but worn — keys polished by use, desk edge rounded by forearms.
```

## B-2 착석 고정 구도 (seated master)

```text
Static frontal frame from directly behind the desk chair, camera height 115cm,
distance 0.9m: desktop, typewriter and window aligned horizontal-vertical, the most
composed frame of the three seats. Paper texture in focus at f/5.6. Faint branch
shadows from the window move imperceptibly across the paper. At night or rain, the
green-shaded lamp confines its warm light (2400K) to the desktop only.
```

## B-3 디테일 컷

```text
Close frame of the typewriter: matte black steel body, the frequently-used keys
subtly polished brighter than the rest, a page with a few typed lines held in the
carriage. Beside it, the green glass shade of the brass lamp. North light, no drama.
```

## 좌석 전용 유의

- 종이는 항상 "몇 줄 타이핑된 한 장". 백지도, 원고 산더미도 금지.
- 창 역광 실루엣 금지 — 북창은 확산광이라 역광 드라마가 물리적으로 없다.
