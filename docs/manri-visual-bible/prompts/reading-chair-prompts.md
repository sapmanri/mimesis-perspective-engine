# C석 — 큰 의자 프롬프트

조립: `[SPACE] + [LOCKS] + 아래 목적별 + (시간·날씨 변형) + [NEGATIVE]`

## C-1 접근 컷 (establishing)

```text
Curving right from the entrance past the east-wall bookshelves at slow walking pace,
camera height 150cm settling toward 105cm: the vertical rhythm of book spines slides
by on the right, then the corner opens — a deep moss-green wool armchair with an
oatmeal blanket, three knee-high book stacks on the floor, the brass floor lamp
(linen shade, 2400K) pooling light over the chair, the small east window with a
deep sill and an ivy plant. The rest of the room stays dim behind.
```

## C-2 착석 고정 구도 (seated master)

```text
Static frame from front-left 30 degrees, camera height 105cm, distance 2.0m — the
farthest and longest-held gaze of the three seats: armchair, blanket, book stacks
and the east window share the frame. f/2.8 allowed here only; focus on the chair,
rain on the window may blur. The blue cloth-bound book rests on the armrest — the
only blue object in the room. Across the room, far out of focus, the fireplace glow
crosses the frame's left edge.
```

## C-3 디테일 컷

```text
Close frame of the armrest: worn moss-green wool with slight pilling on the right
side, the blue cloth book with frayed spine corners, edge of the oatmeal blanket
folded with a lived-in crease. Lamp light from upper left, deep quiet shadow beyond.
```

## 좌석 전용 유의

- 이 자리는 밤·비에서 가장 좋다. 낮 컷에서는 스탠드를 끄고 동창광만으로.
- 감성 스톡사진 문법(담요+머그+책 삼종 클로즈업) 금지 — 디테일 컷도 사용감이 주인공.
