# 카메라 움직임 프롬프트 (영상 생성용)

조립: `[SPACE] + [LOCKS] + 아래 이동 프롬프트 + (시간·날씨) + [NEGATIVE]`
모든 이동은 사람의 걸음(0.7m/s), 가감속 0.8초, 흔들림은 호흡 수준(0.3° 이하).

## M-0 기준 컷 정지 (입장)

```text
Static shot, 3 seconds, from the entrance door at eye height 150cm: the whole room
with its three light pools. Only ambient motion — flame flicker, one dust mote in
the window light. No camera movement at all.
```

## M-A 벽난로로 걸어가 앉기 (4.9s)

```text
Single continuous take, 4.9 seconds: from the entrance, two steps forward onto the
rug, then a gentle leftward arc toward the fireplace corner, walking pace 0.7m/s
with breathing-level sway only. In the final meter the gaze settles first on the
low fire, then the rocking chair enters frame; camera height eases from 150cm down
to 100cm as if sitting. Ends on the A-2 seated composition, then holds still.
```

## M-B 책상으로 걸어가 앉기 (4.4s)

```text
Single continuous take, 4.4 seconds: straight approach from the entrance across the
rug to the desk, the north window growing steadily in the upper frame. Composed,
orderly motion, no lateral drift. Height eases 150cm to 115cm at the chair. Ends on
the B-2 frontal seated composition, then holds still.
```

## M-C 큰 의자로 걸어가 앉기 (5.9s)

```text
Single continuous take, 5.9 seconds: from the entrance, a gentle rightward arc past
the east bookshelves — spines sliding by at arm's length — until the lamp-lit corner
opens; height eases 150cm to 105cm sinking into the chair. Ends on the C-2 seated
composition, then holds still.
```

## M-R 자리 옮김 (좌석 → 좌석)

```text
Single continuous take: rise gently (height up to 150cm over 1s), cross via the
central rug — never along the walls — while the previous seat's light pool leaves
the frame and the next one enters; then the destination seat's approach ending.
Duration = distance / 0.7m/s + 1.6s.
```

## 편집 규칙

- 컷 편집 금지: 좌석 이동은 항상 원테이크.
- 이동 중 다른 좌석의 빛 웅덩이가 최소 1회 프레임을 스쳐야 한다 (같은 방의 증거).
- 착석 후에는 카메라 완전 정지 — 이후의 생동감은 환경 미동의 몫이다.
